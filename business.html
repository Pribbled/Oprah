Harpo Productions

A multimedia production company she founded in 1986. It gave her full control over her content, producing talk shows, films, and documentaries.

OWN: Oprah Winfrey Network

A joint venture with Discovery Communications launched in 2011. It features lifestyle, wellness, and talk-show-style programming.

Oprah.com

A personal development website featuring articles, videos, life advice, and spiritual content. A digital extension of her voice.

O, The Oprah Magazine

A lifestyle magazine launched in 2000 that ran until 2020. It focused on empowerment, mental health, and self-care.

WeightWatchers (WW)

In 2015, Oprah bought a 10% stake in WW and became its spokesperson. She helped transform the brand into a wellness-focused platform.

Oprah’s Book Club

Launched in 1996, it turned dozens of books into bestsellers and boosted the literary industry. It's now integrated with Apple Books.
